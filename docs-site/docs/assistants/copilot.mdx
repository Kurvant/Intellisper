The **Copilot** is Intellisper's in-app AI assistant. It helps you get things done inside the product —
answering questions, helping you build, and guiding you through tasks — right where you're working.

## What it helps with

The Copilot understands the context of what you're doing in Intellisper and helps you move faster:
explaining how something works, helping you assemble an automation, or pointing you to the right place.

## It knows your context

When [Memory](/memory/overview) is enabled and you have [auto-recall](/memory/settings) on, the Copilot
draws on your **personal** and your **org** memory to personalise its answers — so it remembers your
preferences and your team's shared knowledge instead of starting from scratch each time.

This is the same memory the [Agent](/agent/overview) uses. Anything you've taught Intellisper to remember
about how you work informs the Copilot too.

:::note Memory is optional
The Copilot works whether or not memory is on. With memory, it's personalised to you; without it, it
still helps — it just starts each conversation fresh. You control this with your
[auto-recall setting](/memory/settings).
:::
