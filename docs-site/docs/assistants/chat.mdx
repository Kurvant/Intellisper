Chat lets you have a conversation with an AI assistant inside Intellisper. You ask a question or describe
what you need, the assistant responds, and you continue the conversation across as many turns as you like.

## Conversations

Your chats are organised into **conversations**:

- **Start** a new conversation for a new topic.
- **Continue** a conversation across multiple turns — the assistant keeps the thread of what you've
  discussed.
- **Revisit** past conversations to pick up where you left off.
- **Delete** conversations you no longer need.

## During a turn

While the assistant is responding, you stay in control:

- Responses stream back as they're generated, so you see progress immediately.
- You can **stop** a response in progress if you've seen enough or want to redirect.

Long conversations are managed automatically so they keep working smoothly no matter how far they run —
you don't have to think about length limits.

:::note Chat and the Copilot
Chat is a general conversation surface. If you want AI help specifically with building and working inside
Intellisper, the [Copilot](/assistants/copilot) is the assistant tuned for that — it understands your
in-app context.
:::
