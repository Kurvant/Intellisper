The knowledge base lets you upload documents and make their content **searchable by meaning**. Once a
file is in your knowledge base, Intellisper can find the passages relevant to a question — so your
assistants and automations can draw on your own documents, not just their general knowledge.

## What you can do

- **Upload** a document to your project's knowledge base.
- **List** the files you've added.
- **Search** the knowledge base for content relevant to a query.
- **Delete** a file when it's no longer needed.

## How it's used

When you search the knowledge base, Intellisper returns the passages most relevant to your query by
meaning — not just keyword matches. This is what lets an assistant answer a question using the specifics
in your documents, or a flow pull the right reference material as it runs.

Files are scoped to your project, so each project has its own knowledge base.

:::note Getting good results
The knowledge base works best with text-rich documents. Give it the source material your automations and
assistants should be able to reference, and keep it current by removing files that are out of date.
:::
