The agent runs through a Chrome extension connected to your Intellisper workspace. Once it's installed
and your browser is connected, you can start giving it tasks.

<Info>
**You'll need**

An Intellisper **Cloud** or **Enterprise** workspace with the agent included on your plan. If the agent
isn't on your plan yet, you'll see an upgrade prompt when you open it — see [Plans & limits](/agent/plans-and-limits).
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the browser extension">
    Add the Intellisper Agent extension to Chrome and sign in with your workspace account. The extension
    is what performs on-page actions — clicking, typing, navigating — in your live browser session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your browser">
    When the extension is signed in and open, your browser registers as **connected**. The agent can
    only act while your browser is connected: on-page actions happen in your live session, so if the
    extension is closed, any pending work waits until you reconnect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe a task">
    Open the agent and type what you want in plain language — for example, *"Summarise this page,"* or
    *"Find the contact email on this site and save it."* The agent starts a **run** and streams its
    progress back to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch and approve">
    Reading and simple interactions happen automatically. When the agent wants to do something
    consequential — like submitting a form — it **pauses and asks you to approve** before proceeding.
    Approve or reject, and the run continues.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your first task

A good first task is something read-only, so you can see the agent work without any approvals:

> *"Summarise this page in three bullet points."*

The agent reads the current page and streams back a summary. Because reading is a **safe** action,
nothing needs your approval — you'll just see the result appear.

Then try something with an action in it:

> *"Find the newsletter signup on this page and fill in my email, but don't submit it."*

Here the agent will **type** into the field (a reversible action, done automatically) and stop before
**submitting** (a consequential action that needs your approval).

## What happens behind the scenes

Every task you give the agent becomes a **run** — a durable record of the conversation and every action
taken. Runs are streamed live, but they're also saved, so:

- You can close the tab and the run resumes where it left off.
- You can review any past run under [My Agent](/agent/monitoring).
- Admins can see all runs across the workspace under [Agent Activity](/agent/monitoring#agent-activity).
