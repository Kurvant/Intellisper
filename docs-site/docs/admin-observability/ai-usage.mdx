Intellisper gives workspace admins visibility into **how much AI the workspace is using**. AI powers
several features — the agent, the Copilot, chat, and AI-driven flow steps — and this view lets you keep
that consumption and its cost in sight.

## What you can see

- **Spend** — how much AI usage has cost over a period.
- **Usage** — the volume of AI activity across your workspace.

Use it to keep AI costs predictable, spot unexpected spikes, and understand which parts of the product are
driving usage.

## Why it matters

AI usage is metered, and many plans include an allowance of AI credits or capped monthly activity. This
view is how you stay ahead of those limits — so you can decide whether to adjust usage or move to a plan
with more headroom before you hit a cap.

See [Plans & entitlements](/plans/overview) for how AI allowances work across tiers.
