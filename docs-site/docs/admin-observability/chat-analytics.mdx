Chat analytics gives workspace admins an aggregate view of how the AI **chat and assistant** features are
being used across the workspace. It's the observability counterpart to
[AI usage](/admin-observability/ai-usage), focused specifically on conversational AI.

## What it shows

An admin can see workspace-level metrics on chat and assistant activity — how much they're being used and
the trend over time — to understand adoption and demand for these features.

## Why it matters

If your team relies on the Copilot and chat, these metrics show you how much value they're getting and
help you plan capacity. Combined with [AI usage](/admin-observability/ai-usage), they give you the full
picture of AI consumption in your workspace.

:::note Aggregate, not content
Chat analytics reports usage metrics — volumes and trends. It's for understanding *how much* the features
are used, for capacity and planning.
:::
