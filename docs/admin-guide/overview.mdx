The **Platform Admin** is the centralized admin panel for managing your Intellisper instance. It's designed for teams and organizations that want full control over users, integrations, security, and internal automation.

## What Can You Do?

With Platform Admin, you can:

- **Custom Branding:** Tailor the appearance of Intellisper to match your organization's identity, including colors, logos, and fonts.

- **Project Management:** Create, edit, and organize projects for internal teams and users.

- **Block Management:** Control which integration blocks are available, including managing custom or internal blocks for your team's workflows.

- **User Management:** Add and remove users, send invitations, and assign roles and permissions.

- **AI Provider Management:** Configure and manage AI providers (like OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) available for use in your flows.

- **SSO & Security:** Configure Single Sign-On (SSO) providers and manage security settings to ensure your instance is secure.
